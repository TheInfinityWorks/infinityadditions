# How to contribute:
You are free to contribute to the project, even being outside of the core team, but first, you have to read our Guidelines and Code of Conduct. They are in the repository, so don't worry.
### **DON'T BE A PIECE OF SHIT, INSIDE THE PROJECT AND OUTSIDE OF IT.** 

If you are not a person with good human values, morals and ethics, then be aware that you are **NOT** welcome to this project and any of our projects, and you won't be able to participate.

Thanks.
